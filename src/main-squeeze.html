Main Squeeze

Brand Identity

Concept / Design

2018

Main Squeeze is a DJ collective and monthly dance party featuring local Portland talent. The brief was to create a brand identity and graphic system that was modern and flexible but evoked a sense of fun, inclusiveness, and of course, fruit.
